Intro

We are HHMZ Dance School, based in Fourways Dainfern Gauteng SOuth Africa

We teach Hip, Amapiano, Afrobeat, urban Style and Breakdancing. We teach beginner to advanced, competitive classes and non competitive classes.

We have competed in Euro and America , and or soloist in America came 1st and in 2023 we took 3 groups to Croatia and they made top 10. We have won a lot of competitive and locally and done a few music videos and work with Co-operate events.

Our story

Our founder Henry Nene was seen dancing when he used to work at a restaurant and the clients approached him and asked him to teacher their kids and that how HHMZ was born.

Our story

Our founder Henry Nene was seen dancing when he used to work at a restaurant and the clients approached him and asked him to teacher their kids and that how HHMZ was born.

Our team

We won a hip jop solo in LA America with our student Asemahle who came 1st for her hip hop and 3rd for her open.

We took 3 groups to Croatia for World dance masters and all the groups made top 10.

We have won a lot competitions locally and done music video and co-operate events.

Our services

We teacher dance from the ages of 4 to adults (Hip Hop, Amapiano, Urban Style , Afrobeat, Breakdance).

We have a clothing brand which means Design It Yourself, we make out own outfits for dance and for fashion.

We also offer fun classes, for people that want ot come and learn TikTok dances.

We offer professional performances for music videos, events and competitions .
